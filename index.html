FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know about Classic Reach for coating shops.

How quickly can my AI Coating Assistant start working?
Most shops are live within 1–2 weeks. We handle the entire setup — learning your coating packages and warranty terms, configuration, testing, and launch. From day one, your assistant is replying to quote requests, logging installs, and confirming bay appointments.
How is this different from a generic chatbot?
A generic chatbot reads from a script and collects contact info. Classic Reach is built around the coating warranty lifecycle — it tracks install dates and warranty length, sends month-11 annual maintenance reminders, quotes coating and PPF packages, and books the visit. Generic AI replies to leads; Classic Reach protects repeat revenue.
Can it connect to my existing CRM and scheduling software?
Yes. We integrate with most major CRMs, scheduling platforms, and business tools. If you're on GoHighLevel, a custom CRM, or any standard scheduling platform — we wire it in. Custom integrations are available for legacy systems.
Does it report installs to CARFAX or pull vehicle history?
Not today. Classic Reach is future-ready for vehicle-history and CARFAX-style reporting workflows if the required integrations become available — but we don't present those as live features. What works now is warranty tracking, annual maintenance reminders, quoting, documentation, and review requests.
What ongoing support is included?
Every plan includes ongoing monitoring, optimization, and support. We tune your AI Coating Assistant as your shop evolves, adjust automations based on results, and you have direct access to our team. This isn't set-it-and-forget-it — we stay involved.
